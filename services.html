Services

We’re Here to Serve You.

How do we deliver the best service to our customers? By making wholesome, quality products, standing by our values, and meeting the needs of each customer individually. We don’t just say we provide the best service, we’ll show you what great service looks like.

From customer service to delivery, here are the value-added services we offer.

Delivery

Fresh from our ovens to your kitchens — six days a week.

Whether you’re ordering 10,000 Artisan Rolls as part of a standard shipment or 10 Coconut Layer Cakes for a special corporate event, we deliver fresh Monday through Saturday until the last order is fulfilled. Standard delivery rates apply for the Twin Cities and surrounding metro area; call for pricing if you fall outside of this range. Orders placed before 2pm are delivered as early as next day. For more information on delivery please see our FAQs section.

Sometimes, you need it yesterday. We get it, it happens. In that case we’re happy to work with you on custom and rush deliveries. Call or contact us for more information.

Sampling

It’s like Saturday at the supermarket — without the crowds.

We fully stand behind each and every one of our products and strongly believe in the “try before you buy” philosophy of sampling. Whether it’s an item you haven’t previously ordered from us, or a new or seasonal product we’ve just released, we are more than willing to sample it for you before you commit to a full order. Any product, any time.

We offer bakery tours on request and are happy to prepare samples for your visit, or contact us to set up a time and we’ll visit you!

Custom Formulation

Need more? We’re at your service.

Mainstreet Bakery is a full-service bakery capable of making a variety of custom and special order products. We love to help customers find solutions and are happy to talk with you about your specific custom needs. Find out more about our Custom Formulation capabilities or contact us to talk about your needs.
